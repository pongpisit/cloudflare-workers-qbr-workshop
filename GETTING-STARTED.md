## Key Terms Explained

### Worker
**What it is:** Code that runs on Cloudflare's servers  
**Think of it as:** A mini-program that responds to web requests  
**Example:** When someone visits your website, your Worker sends them the page

### R2 Storage
**What it is:** Cloud storage for files  
**Think of it as:** Dropbox or Google Drive  
**Example:** Store profile pictures, photos, videos

### D1 Database
**What it is:** A place to store structured data  
**Think of it as:** Excel spreadsheet in the cloud  
**Example:** Store user information, photo captions, comments

### Workers AI
**What it is:** AI models you can use  
**Think of it as:** ChatGPT that you can add to your apps  
**Example:** Chatbots, image generation, text analysis

### Dashboard
**What it is:** Website where you control everything  
**Think of it as:** Control panel for your apps  
**Example:** Create Workers, upload files, view statistics

### Binding
**What it is:** Connecting your Worker to other services  
**Think of it as:** Plugging in a USB cable  
**Example:** Connect your Worker to R2 storage or D1 database

### Deploy
**What it is:** Making your code live on the internet  
**Think of it as:** Publishing your work  
**Example:** Click "Deploy" and your app is instantly online
